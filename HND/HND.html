3D Animation

I created this animation over a span of a month, the idea was a 3D remake of my favourite game Pokémon Mystery Dungeon it was to have 4 major scenes, Stormy Sea, Beach at Dusk, Montage (Which got scraped) & Treeshroud Forest

I thought we'd get a few months to do all this but no, so I spent 2 weeks of non-stop working - literally, wake up, work on animation, go to bed, repeat - In the end I had to remove 2 days of editing due copyright issues with the heavy use of pokemon *names* - but them looking like pokemon is perfectly fine?? - which was... fun to get back. There's a few things I'd change - cheif among them the UVing - but for what it is, it's quite good

I created created 3 main Models: Mudkip, Cyndaquil & Groyvle

Graded Unit - Rooftop Runner

I made a proposal outlining every little detail: Software, Design Methodology, Colour palette (which, fun fact I reused for this site!), etc. The original game was meant to be 2D endless runner but after a quick chat with one of my classmates compared it to Clustertruck and that gave me the idea to make it 3D... with less than a hour I made it so and I think it's much better for it! Sadly with the changes some ideas had to be dropped... namely the whole "Zombie Apocalypse" part of it... but thankfully we weren't graded on how well it adhered to the brief.

I made this for my graded unit, We were to choose from 2 briefs: "Apocalypse" or "Time Travel" and I choose Apocalypse
